About The Daily Diss-patch

The Daily Diss-patch is a satire and news-commentary site covering finance, politics, military headlines, and the general chaos of the internet. We blend curated headlines from established outlets with original satirical commentary, parody articles, and humor features.

What we publish

Original satire, parody news articles, commentary on real headlines, and entertainment features such as satirical horoscopes and "Vegas odds" on current events. Curated headlines link back to their original publishers, who retain all rights to their work.

Editorial note

Satirical content on this site is fictional and intended for entertainment. It should not be relied on as factual reporting, financial advice, or any other kind of advice. When we link to real reporting, the linked publisher is the source of record.
